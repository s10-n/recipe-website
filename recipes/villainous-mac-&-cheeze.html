Villainous Mac & Cheeze

[Ingredients] [Directions]

Tags: macaroni, cheese, pasta, baked, casserole,

  • Serves: 4
  • Prep time: 15 minutes
  • Cook time: 1 hour

Directions [^]

  1. Cook the macaroni until done as usual, adding sugar and garlic to water. Do not overcook it. Drain macaroni in strainer.
  2. Place macaroni, butter, salt, pepper, milk, Colby/jack cheese, and 3 cups of cheddar cheese in an oven-safe pot. Boil slowly, and when done, add bread crumbs.
  3. Cover everything with aluminum foil and cook for 45 minutes at 350.
  4. Uncover and add extra cup of cheddar cheese across the top. Bake uncovered for another 15 minutes.
